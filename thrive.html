Field Research

Problem Statement

Less than half of US adults meet the recommended 150 minutes of weekly aerobic activity; only about 47% do so in 2025.

Opportunity Statement

How might we use AI and emerging tech to close gaps in health outcomes and access—reducing communication barriers, boosting motivation, and delivering personalized, inclusive experiences?

Solution

THRIVE transforms daily health behaviors into playful, bite-sized quests. Gamified missions map to AHA's Life's Essential 8, while an AI assistant personalizes plans, nudges, and recommendations.

Persona

Journey Map

Insights from the journey map informed the development of Thrive’s key features, addressing motivation gaps and accessibility barriers.

Visual Design System

The color palette and typography were designed to convey positivity, motivation, and inclusivity— aligning with AHA's brand tone and the app's mission to make healthy living approachable.

Key Features

  • Onboarding & Personalization — conversational intake + tailored goals
  • Game Center — micro-challenges aligned to Life’s Essential 8:
    • Weight Balance (Manage Weight), Mission Rush (Be More Active)
    • Food Sorter (Eat Better), Pressure Balance (Manage Blood Pressure)
    • Calm Pulse (Healthy Sleep), Pipe Clean (Control Cholesterol)
    • Sweet Catcher (Manage Blood Sugar), Clear Lungs (Quit Tobacco)
  • AI Health Companion — reduces access barriers; personalized nudges
  • Rewards Store — earn/redeem points to sustain motivation
  • Community & Social — inclusive challenges and peer support
  • Home Dashboard — daily missions, streaks, progress

    • Why It's Different

    • Community-centric design that fosters belonging and shared progress
    • Inclusive gamification across abilities and access levels
    • Rewards tied to meaningful, health-aligned behaviors
    • AI assistance that personalizes plans and demystifies health info
