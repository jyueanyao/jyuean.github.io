Problem Statement

Caregivers in elderly care facilities face significant challenges: no real-time visibility of residents' locations, high stress from manual supervision, and inconsistent data during shift handovers. These gaps lead to safety risks and burnout among staff.

Opportunity Statement

How might we use IoT technology to provide caregivers with reliable, real-time monitoring while maintaining residents' dignity and autonomy?

Solution

TechCare is a BLE-based monitoring system that tracks resident locations and wellbeing in real-time, helping caregivers respond quickly to emergencies while reducing manual workload and stress.

Persona

Journey Map

The journey map illustrates a caregiver's daily workflow, highlighting pain points during shift changes, emergency responses, and routine monitoring tasks.

Information Architecture

The system is organized around three main user roles (Caregivers, Administrators, and Residents) with a clear navigation structure that prioritizes real-time information access and emergency response capabilities.
